My services

Full Body

Includes work on your back, arms, legs, feet, hands, head, neck, and shoulders.

Foot massage

Foot massage is the application of pressure onto particular areas of the soles of the feet.

Body Wrap

Also known as a 'body cocoon' or 'body mask', is a spa treatment created to enhance the health and appearance of skin.

Swedish massage

As the best-known type of bodywork performed today, one of the primary goals of the Swedish massage technique is to relax the entire body.

Deep Tissue

Similar to Swedish massage, but the deeper pressure is beneficial in releasing chronic muscle tension.

Facial
